Data Deletion Policy for KAI

Effective Date: September 7, 2025

How Users Can Delete Their Data

KAI does not store any of your personal or financial data on external servers. All information (such as SMS messages processed and expense records) is stored locally on your device only.

You can permanently delete all your data by:

  • Uninstalling the KAI app from your device
  • Or, clearing the app's storage and cache from your device settings

Types of Data Deleted

When you uninstall or clear storage, the following data is deleted immediately:

  • All SMS transaction records processed by the app
  • All manually added expense entries
  • All app settings and preferences

Retention

No copies of your data are retained by the developer. All data remains on your device until you choose to delete it as described above.
